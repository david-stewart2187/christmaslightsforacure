What is Type 1 Diabetes?

Definition:

Type 1 Diabetes (T1D) manifests itself after your body falsely identifies your pancreas as "bad tissue", and proceeds to destroy it.

Symptoms:

Some of the symptoms for T1D can be excessive thirst and hunger, sudden mood changes, blurry vision, fatigue, and a high blood sugar.

Prevention of T1D:

Unfortunately, Type 1 Diabetes can be triggered by genetics and environmental factors, so there isn't any efficient way to prevent it. There isn't a cure yet, but in recent years, different studies from around the country have found ways to delay the onset of T1D.
